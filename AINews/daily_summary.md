* Congress has stalled on producing federal regulations, allowing states to take the lead in addressing AI-related issues such as intellectual property rights and free speech.
* The article questions the privatization of humanity's informational heritage through monetizing AI models, which rely on publicly available data and content created by individuals.

* Concerns are rising that AI advancements may undermine human independence and critical thinking, with potential acceleration of conspiracism and disinformation through generative models.
